# 📘 How to Use Verixa

This guide provides operational instructions across all three roles within the Verixa Ecosystem.

---

## 👤 For Clients (Immigration Seekers)

**1. Finding a Consultant**
- Navigate to the **Home** or **Search Directory**.
- Use the advanced filters to isolate parameters like "Province", "Language", or "Consultant Status".
- Click any profile card to view their "Money Page" (Reviews, Credentials, and Services).

**2. Booking a Session**
- On a Consultant Profile, select an offered service block (e.g., "30 Min Discovery").
- The **5-Step Engine** launches.
- Select your time, input your case details, and submit. *Note: The request is sent, but must be reviewed by the Consultant to be completely valid.*

**3. Tracking Your Request**
- You will be redirected to the "Post-Checkout" tracker.
- Head to **Dashboard -> My Bookings** to view the status timeline. 
- When the timeline moves from `Pending` -> `Confirmed`, the Consultant's Meeting Link will appear via a massive actionable button.

---

## 🏢 For Consultants (Immigration Professionals)

**1. Claiming & Setting Up**
- Search for your License Number and hit **Claim This Profile**.
- Complete Verification Steps (Email / ID checking).
- Once logged in, visit **Dashboard -> Settings** to configure your core Profile, standard Rates, and open Weekly Availability matrix.

**2. Configuring Meeting Rules**
- Crucially, open the **Meeting Setup** toggle in your settings.
- Select your default mechanism (Video Call vs Call vs In-Person).
- Paste your standard URL (e.g., your personal Zoom or Meet room) and Join Instructions. *This saves you from typing it repeatedly.*

**3. Managing the Action Inbox**
- Verixa brings urgent matters to you. Open **Dashboard Home**.
- Under "Action Required", look for `Pending Requests` or items needing Links.
- Click **Accept**. The modal pre-fills with your default Meeting Setup. Verify it, click "Confirm", and the client is automatically emailed the hook.

---

## 👑 For Platform Administrators

**1. The Command Center**
- Login using an account carrying the `ADMIN` role.
- Upon hitting `/dashboard`, you will be immediately routed to the customized Dark-Mode **Global Dashboard**.

**2. Resolving Disputes**
- Click **Support & Tickets** in your sidebar.
- You can view and reply to any open ticket spawned by Clients or Consultants. When the issue is resolved, update the Status to `CLOSED` to lock the thread.

**3. Financial Oversight & Global Audits**
- Click **Bookings**. You'll see a unified table of every transaction regardless of status.
- Clicking a Booking ID links to the **Deep Audit** view.
- This read-only interface breaks down the complete Financial Split (Gross Cents vs Platform Cut vs Net Consultant Income) and displays a chronological log of all interactions ever tied to that slot.
